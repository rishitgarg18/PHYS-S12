### Week 11: Wildcard This week was supposed to be the wildcard week. I used this week's time in the lab to produce an MIT logo through a water-jet cutter. Here are some photos: ![water jet photo](waterjet2.jpeg) ![mit logo](mitlogo.jpeg) ![mit logo 2](mitlogo2.jpeg) <video controls> <source src="waterjet.mp4" type="video/mp4"> </video> Other than this, I made progress on my Final Project. My Final Project is going to be a smart sole [insole] that can be inserted into any running shoe. The smart sole focuses on a few very important metrics for any runner. The Smart Sole focuses on the following metrics: position of the shoe in 3-D, acceleration, and temperature. The Smart Sole employs an ESP32 Feather Huzzah chip along with a gyroscope as its main component.
