### Week 10: Machine Building This week we used stepper motors to work to build on the work we had done in our assignment for servo motors. Using stepper motor control, we weare able to control the way our stepper motors move, also achieveing increased accuracy. To use a stepper motor, the following materials are required: a breadboard, jumper wires, Arduino UNO/Metro, a 100 microfarad filament, and most importantly, a stepper motor and a motor driver. For this week's assignment, I used the STSPIN220 motor driver.
